### Laylatul Qadr — The Night of Power

**لَيْلَةِ الْقَدْرِ**

Laylatul Qadr, or the "Night of Power" or "Night of Decree," is the holiest night in the Islamic calendar, commemorating when the first verses of the Qur'an were revealed to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). It occurs during the last ten nights of Ramadan, specifically on an odd-numbered night, and is considered better than **1,000 months of worship**.
